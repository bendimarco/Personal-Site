About Me

Hello! I am Tomas Germanas, a Junior at Centennial High School. The piano has always been a major part of my life, and I started playing when I was 5 years old. Since then, I sought to apply my music skills and interests in as many ways as I could. My first time branching out was learning the clarinet, and I am the 2nd chair clarinet player of the Centennial High School Wind Ensemble. I also volunteer in various music-related activities. I am a member of the Tri-M Music Honor Society and a member of the board of the Music Mentors Club, where I provide free, supplemental lessons to students at Burleigh Manor Middle School. Now, my most recent project is “I’ve Got Your Bach”, and I am committed to providing quality accompaniment to students at a low price.

What is I’ve got your Bach?

I’ve got your Bach is a company that provides affordable, reliable, exceptional piano accompaniment from an experienced pianist myself, Tomas. I am available to accompany your recital, music exam, lesson, rehearsal, etc. So, why should you choose me to accompany you?

Quality

I have over 10 years of experience in piano. I have performed highly in national level musical competitions and performed at renowned locations like Carnegie Hall.

Affordability

I’ve got your Bach provides affordable and adaptable pricing. You only pay for what you need!

Proven Success

All the students that I have accompanied have received 1’s at the county level.
